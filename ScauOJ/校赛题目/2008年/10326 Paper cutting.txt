10326 Paper cutting

时间限制:1000MS  内存限制:65535K
提交次数:1 通过次数:0

题型: 编程题   语言: 无限制
Description

You are now given a large sheet of paper, which is in rectangle shape. Now you are asked to cut another rectangle from the 
given paper. Your task is to decide whether is it possible to cut the rectangle from the given paper.



输入格式

The input consists of T test cases. The number of them (T) is given on the first line of the input file. Each test case 
consists of a single line. The line contains exactly four integer numbers separated by spaces: A, B, X and Y. A and B 
indicate the dimensions of the given paper, X and Y are the dimensions of the requested rectangle (1 <= A,B,X,Y <= 50000). 


输出格式

Your task is to determine whether it is possible to cut the rectangle from the given paper. If so, you are to print one 
line with the sentence "Cutting is possible." Otherwise print the sentence " Cutting is impossible ". 


输入样例

2
10 10 8 8
8 8 10 10


输出样例

Cutting is possible.
Cutting is impossible.
